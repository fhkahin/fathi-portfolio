About Me

I am a Biomedical Scientist, Web Developer, and Refugee Case Worker with a unique blend of expertise and a passion for making a difference. In my role as a case worker, I support refugees and asylum seekers, helping them integrate into society while navigating complex challenges. This work has strengthened my empathy and my drive to create positive, lasting change. In addition to my humanitarian efforts, I have developed a strong foundation in technology, particularly in the Fullstack PERN (PostgreSQL, Express, React, Node.js) stack. I have hands-on experience with the core components of the stack, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, all of which are integrated into the PERN stack for building dynamic, data-driven web applications. My technical journey is one of continuous learning, and I am driven by the belief that technology has the power to transform lives. I see the intersection of my experience in biomedical science, refugee support, and web development as an opportunity to bring fresh perspectives to the tech industry. With a strong commitment to both humanitarian work and technological innovation, I am eager to leverage my diverse skills to contribute to projects that make a meaningful and lasting impact.
